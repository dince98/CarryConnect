Our Story

Who We Are

CarryConnect is a fresh, new project born from a simple idea: what if skilled gamers could help others reach their goals while maintaining complete account safety and transparency? We're building a community where friends help each other climb ranks, and we're excited to grow it into something special.

We're a dedicated team of professional gamers across multiple titles including League of Legends, Valorant, and Roblox. Each member of our team has achieved top rankings in their respective games and understands what it takes to climb efficiently and safely.

Unlike larger, impersonal boosting services, we're creating a passionate collective. We aim to know our clients by name, understand their specific goals, and treat every account with the same care we'd give our own.

Our philosophy is simple: no bots, no scripts, just skill. Every boost is performed manually by our team members, using stream-proof methods to ensure your account's safety. We believe in building relationships with our clients, not just completing transactions.

Our Core Values

Security First

Your account safety is non-negotiable. We use VPNs, stream-proof methods, and never ask for unnecessary information.

Transparency

No hidden fees, no bots, no shortcuts. We communicate clearly about timelines, methods, and pricing.

Community Focus

We're gamers helping gamers. We offer coaching, advice, and build lasting relationships with our clients.
