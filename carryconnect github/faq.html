How Can We Help?

We've compiled the most common questions from our community. If you can't find what you're looking for, our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.

General Questions
What is CarryConnect?
CarryConnect is a manual game boosting service run by passionate gamers. We help players reach their desired ranks in games like League of Legends, Valorant, and Roblox. No bots, no scripts—just pure skill and dedication.
Which games do you support?
We currently offer boosting for League of Legends, Valorant, and Roblox. This includes rank boosting, placement matches, farming services, and coaching sessions. More games coming soon!
How long does a boost take?
Delivery time varies based on the game, current rank, and desired rank. Most League of Legends boosts (e.g., Gold to Platinum) are completed within 1-3 days. You'll receive an estimated timeframe before ordering and updates throughout the process.
Safety & Security
Is my account safe?
Account safety is our top priority. We use VPNs that match your region, never chat with friends, never modify account details, and all boosters sign NDAs. We've completed thousands of orders with zero account issues.
Do you use bots or cheats?
Never. Every order is done manually by real, high-ranked players. No bots, no scripts, no cheats — just pure skill and game knowledge. We're gamers ourselves and take pride in honest work.
Will Riot/Epic/Roblox ban my account?
Boosting is against terms of service, but we take every precaution to avoid detection: natural play patterns, no win-trading, and region-matched VPNs. Our methods are discreet and proven safe over thousands of orders.
Payments
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency (BTC/ETH). All payments are processed securely through encrypted gateways.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes, we offer a full refund if we cannot start your order within 48 hours. Partial refunds may be available if you cancel mid-boost. See our Refund Policy for complete details.
When do I pay?
Payment is required upfront to secure your order and booster. You'll only be asked for login details after payment is confirmed. We use secure payment processors to protect your information.
Boosting Process
How do I place an order?
Select your game, current rank, desired rank, and any extras like streaming or priority order. Complete checkout, and we'll match you with an available booster within 15 minutes. You'll receive confirmation and estimated start time.
Can I play with the booster?
Yes! For most games, you can select "duo boost" where you play alongside our pro. For solo boosting, we play on your account while you're offline. Both options are safe and effective.
Can I get updates during the boost?
Absolutely! You'll receive email/SMS updates, or you can check progress live on our dashboard. You can also message support anytime for status updates.
Warranty & Support
Do you offer a rank warranty?
Yes! We provide a 72-hour free refill warranty. If you drop below your target rank within 72 hours of completion, we'll boost you back up at no cost. Just contact support with proof.
What if the booster loses games?
Our boosters maintain a high win rate (80%+), but occasional losses happen. We track progress and extend playtime as needed to ensure you reach your target rank. No extra charges, ever.
How do I contact support?
Our support team is available 24/7 via Discord, email (support@carryconnect.com), or through our contact page. We typically reply within minutes, not hours.
