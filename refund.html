Refund Policy

Fair and transparent refunds for our boosting services.

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Our Commitment to Fairness

At CarryConnect, we want you to be completely satisfied with your boosting experience. We've designed our refund policy to be fair and transparent, protecting both our customers and our boosters. Below you'll find the specific circumstances under which refunds are issued.

Full Refund

If we cannot start your order within 48 hours

Partial Refund

If you cancel mid-boost (unused portion)

No Refund

After successful completion or warranty period

1. Full Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a 100% full refund in the following situations:

  • Order not started: If we cannot assign a booster and begin your order within 48 hours of purchase (excluding weekends and holidays).
  • Technical issues: If a critical technical problem on our end prevents us from delivering the service (e.g., website errors that corrupt your order).
  • Duplicate payment: If you accidentally paid twice for the same order (we'll refund the duplicate).

Full refunds cover the entire amount paid, including any taxes or fees.

2. Partial Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a partial refund in these circumstances:

  • Cancellation mid-boost: If you request to cancel an order after it has started but before completion, we will refund the unused portion based on progress completed. For example, if 40% of the boost is done, you'll receive a 60% refund.
  • Significant delays: If your order exceeds the estimated completion time by more than 72 hours and you choose to cancel rather than wait, you may receive a partial refund (typically 50% of the remaining value).
  • Booster performance issues: If you experience consistent problems with a booster (e.g., poor communication, excessive losses) and we cannot reassign a replacement promptly, you may receive a partial refund for the affected portion.

Partial refund amounts are calculated at our discretion based on the specific circumstances and progress data.

3. No Refund Eligibility

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

  • Completed orders: Once your boost has been successfully completed and you've confirmed satisfaction (or 24 hours pass without dispute), no refund is available.
  • Change of mind: If you simply change your mind after the order has started, no refund is available (partial refunds may apply if you cancel—see Section 2).
  • Game publisher actions: We do not refund for account bans, suspensions, or penalties imposed by game publishers (Riot, Epic, Roblox, etc.), as these are beyond our control. However, if our negligence caused the action, we will work with you to resolve it.
  • Post-warranty rank loss: After the 72-hour warranty period expires, we are not obligated to refund for rank loss (though we may offer discounted re-boosting).
  • Chargebacks: If you initiate a chargeback with your bank or payment processor without first contacting us, you forfeit any right to a refund under this policy, and your account may be permanently banned.

4. 72-Hour Rank Warranty

Free refill warranty: We offer a 72-hour free refill warranty on all completed boosts. If you drop below your target rank within 72 hours of completion, we will boost you back up at no cost. This warranty covers:

  • Rank decay due to inactivity or losses
  • Losses that drop you below the guaranteed rank

To claim: Contact us through our contact page within 72 hours of completion with your order number and proof (screenshots) of your current rank. We'll start the free refill within 24 hours.

Note: The warranty applies only to the specific game and queue you purchased. It does not cover other game modes or accounts.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to our contact page.
  2. Provide your full name, order number (if available), and the email address used for the purchase.
  3. Clearly state that you are requesting a refund and explain the reason.
  4. If applicable, attach any supporting evidence (e.g., screenshots, communication logs).

We will acknowledge your request within 24 hours and process it within 5-7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

6. Refund Processing Timeline

Once approved, refunds are processed as follows:

  • Credit/debit cards: 5-10 business days, depending on your bank.
  • PayPal: 2-5 business days.
  • Cryptocurrency: 1-3 business days (refunded in the same cryptocurrency at the current exchange rate).

You will receive a confirmation email when the refund is initiated.

7. Disputes and Chargebacks

If you believe a refund is warranted, please contact us first. Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute without contacting us:

  • May result in permanent account suspension
  • Forfeits your eligibility for future services
  • Does not guarantee a faster resolution (in fact, it often delays the process)

We are committed to resolving issues amicably. Please give us the opportunity to address your concerns before taking external action.

8. Promotional Credits and Discounts

Refunds for orders that used promotional credits or discounts will be calculated as follows:

  • If you paid partially with credits, the refund will first cover the paid portion, then credits (if applicable).
  • Promotional credits used on a refunded order may be restored at our discretion.
  • Discount codes are non-refundable (the discount itself has no cash value).

9. Exceptions

In rare circumstances, we may make exceptions to this policy at our sole discretion. Factors considered include:

  • Long-standing customer history
  • Extenuating circumstances (e.g., medical emergencies, technical glitches)
  • Good faith efforts to resolve issues

Any exceptions granted are one-time and do not modify the general policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or need assistance with a refund request, please reach out through our contact page.

Summary: We want you to be happy with your purchase. If something goes wrong, we'll make it right—whether through a refund, free refill, or other resolution. Contact us promptly, and we'll work with you to find a fair solution.
